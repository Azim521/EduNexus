👋 Our Story

Four students.
One big idea.

EduNexus was born out of a real problem we faced ourselves — not being able to find the right help at the right time. So we decided to build the solution.

What Drives Us
Our Mission & Vision
🎯

Our Mission

To eliminate last-minute academic panic by connecting every LPU student with the right peer tutor instantly — making quality academic help affordable, flexible, and campus-native.

🌟

Our Vision

To become India's go-to peer learning platform — where every student finds a trusted, verified tutor in minutes, and every talented peer earns fairly for the knowledge they share.

Our Story
Born from a real student problem

We've all been there — the night before an exam, stuck on a concept, nowhere to turn. Existing platforms offer recorded courses but none of them solve the urgent problem.

We surveyed students across LPU and quickly confirmed what we already knew: students need instant, one-on-one academic help — not another video library.

So we built EduNexus — a smart platform that matches students with peer tutors in seconds, lets them try a free demo, and charges only per session. Starting at LPU, built for India.
